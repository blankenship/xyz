I’m a multi-disciplinary designer, writer, and instigator for teams. I’m devoted to advancing the truth that brand and product are not disassociated disciplines, but overlap in the heart and mind of one person interacting with what we make. You can find my lines of business for brand and interface design at blankenship.xyz.
