Blankenship Studio We help in-house design teams, startups, and small businesses build and refine their visual and verbal brand identity. From naming to strategy, editorial to environments, products to wayfinding, we’re your man, Zatara.

We Are

We are interested in the intersection of design and business, not just aesthetics. We design flexible visual and verbal identities, environments, programs of signage, wayfinding systems, icons, illustrations, publications, and small websites.

We Aren’t

We aren’t a full service agency, an e-commerce web shop, or a social media marketing firm. We don’t just execute decisions you’ve already made. We’re not a vendor; we’re your partner. We ask questions, collaborate, and design together.

You Are

You are out here making a difference, serving people. We love helping organizations we believe should exist. We’ve partnered with big companies, small businesses, non-profits, startups, architects, contractors, neighborhoods, and moms and pops.

We’d love to collaborate with you and your team.
