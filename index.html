Bienvenidos a blankenship.xyz

Hello. I’m your host — Joshua Blankenship. Ready, set, internet.

Here's the scoop I’m a 40yo human in Greenville, South Carolina, known for blogging *kind of a lot*, designing brand identities, writing and illustrating books and paper goods with my lovely wife, making affordable logos for small businesses, and thinking about Magic: the Gathering way too much. This little website is a place to aggregate all those endeavors — a one-stop Blankenshop.

I’m a designer by trade, which means different things on different days, but generally speaking I’m passionate about brand identity, the intersection of design and business, equipping in-house teams with foundational, sustainable design systems, and training the next generation of designers to go further than I ever have.

I’m also a magician. And by “magician” I mean I’m absurdly addicted to Magic:the Gathering, which is objectively the greatest game ever created. I play a format called Commander (or Elder Dragon Highlander for the old school nerds), and host a monthly gathering or like-minded collectible card game enthusiastics.

Currently I am exploring my next client projects (which may or may not include a food service startup helping McDonald’s get on track and a Portland-based pediatric care startup reimagining kid care), teaching Graphic Design 101 at Furman Unversity, illustrating 10 Awesome Things a counting book for kids, and planning a fence and firepit for our backyard.
