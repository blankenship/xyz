Bienvenidos a blankenship.xyz

Hello. I’m your host
— Joshua Blankenship.
Ready, set, internet.

Here’s the scoop I’m a 40yo human in Greenville, South Carolina, known for blogging *kind of a lot*, designing brand identities, writing and illustrating books and paper goods with my family, making affordable logos for small businesses, and thinking about Magic: the Gathering way too much. This little website is a place to aggregate all those endeavors — like a one-stop Blankenshop.

I’m a designer by trade, which means different things on different days, but generally speaking I’m passionate about brand identity, the intersection of design and business, weird logos, equipping in-house teams with sustainable design systems, and teaching, mentoring, and coaching new designers to go further than I ever will.

I play Magic: the Gathering which is objectively the greatest game ever created. I’m a fan of a 100-card singleton format called Commander (or Elder Dragon Highlander for the old school nerds), and host a monthly gathering and Slack channel of like-minded collectible card game enthusiasts here in Greenville.

Currently I am exploring my next client projects (which may or may not include a food service startup helping McDonald’s get on track and a Portland-based pediatric care startup reimagining kid care), teaching Graphic Design 101 at Furman Unversity, illustrating 10 Awesome Things a counting book for kids, and planning a fence and firepit for our backyard.

Other things you might care about include Dribbble, some old music I’ve written or new songs I post on my Instagram Stories, non-Haiku poetry, some of the food we cook and hashtag #blankenkitchen on Instagram, the occasional conference talk I do like this or this, and my absurdly good taste in playlists. Thanks for stopping by. Onward and upward.
